RISKS

Communication between our group members is important since it will help us to keep track of our processes and works. In that case, our group will use Messenger as the main way to communicate with each other from far away since all of us are using Facebook a lot. Another thing that we will also use to allocate tasks to each member and keep track of our project is Trello. This application will show what each member is doing and what tasks have been done. We will also provide phone numbers to each other in order to call if there are members going offline or have not seen the messages on Facebook for too long (about 4 hours). Our meetings will mainly take place at school about 2 times a week, and maybe more depending on the amount of work in that week. We choose meeting face-to-face as our main approach with the purpose of being more convenient to communicate with each other. Also, if we cannot meet at school in that week for whatever reasons, then video chatting on Messenger will be a good approach in this case. Another thing to plan is that if there are members who are missing for the following reasons:

Coding problem: Beside the truth that this is an expensive technology, our group is having one more problem, it is about the coding section for the AI. As we are newbies and this technology is also new to us, so we don’t have any knowledge about this technology as the coding and the function of the AI. Therefore, it will affect our outcomes at the end of the project. For example, the application may not work very well, or errors may occur because the code is missing.

Time consuming: To train an AI, we need to collect a lot of information for the AI. For example, if you want the machine to recognize an animal, you need to collect a lot of pictures about that animal, so that the machine can recognize it more accurately. Therefore, it will take us a lot of time to get enough data for the AI, because this technology is new to every member in our group. This problem does not just occur for newbies like us, but many IT professionals still find it hard to collect enough data for their project.

Problems related to data: As we know, AI is a costly and newest technology nowadays. But there are still some drawbacks or limitations of the AI. For example, the AI cannot think by itself, the machine only does whatever you programmed it to do. Therefore, you need to carefully choose the right data to insert into the machine. If you don’t have enough data for the AI to learn, therefore it can lead to many mistakes in using the machine. For example, you want the AI to recognize a dog in front of it, but it gives the result as a cat. Moreover, lack of skilled people can also lead to this problem, especially the newbies like us. Because the AI also needs to be coded properly and correctly.
